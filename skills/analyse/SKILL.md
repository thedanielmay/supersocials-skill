# SuperSocials: Analyse

Think like a growth strategist. Turn numbers into decisions.

## What This Module Produces

- **What's working** — patterns across high-performing content
- **What's not** — patterns across low-performing content
- **3 next actions** — concrete, platform-specific, prioritised

## Process

### Step 1: Get the data

Ask the user to paste their metrics. Accept any format — screenshots described in text, native platform exports, or manually typed numbers. Useful data:

- Platform
- Time period
- Reach or impressions
- Engagement rate (or likes + comments + shares + follower count to calculate it)
- Top 3 posts (format, topic, engagement)
- Any posts you noticed underperformed (optional — most platforms don't surface bottom performers)
- Follower count change over period

If they can only share some of this, work with what's there. Don't refuse to analyse incomplete data — note what's missing and what it limits. Bottom-performer data is helpful but not required; top-performer patterns are usually sufficient to identify what to stop doing.

### Step 2: Analyse

Look for patterns, not individual data points. One bad post proves nothing. Three bad posts of the same format is a pattern.

Questions to answer:
- Which content format is outperforming? (Reels vs carousels vs Stories, etc.)
- Which pillar/topic is resonating most?
- What's the engagement rate vs platform benchmark? (Instagram: 1–3% good, 3%+ strong; LinkedIn: 2–5%; TikTok: 5–10%)
- Is reach growing, flat, or declining?
- Is follower growth correlated with specific post types?

### Step 3: Pressure-test the interpretation (announce before doing)

> "Calling `expert-panel` to get a second opinion on this interpretation before I give you my recommendations."

Invoke the `expert-panel` skill with your draft analysis. Ask the panel: are there alternative explanations for the data? Are the recommended actions sound? What's being missed?

Fold must-fix feedback into the analysis. Deliver the final verdict — not the panel debate.

### Step 4: Deliver

Output format:

```
--- ANALYSIS: [Platform] — [Time Period] ---

WHAT'S WORKING
[Pattern 1]: [Specific evidence from data]
[Pattern 2]: [Specific evidence from data]

WHAT'S NOT
[Pattern 1]: [Specific evidence from data]
[Pattern 2]: [Specific evidence from data]

3 NEXT ACTIONS
1. [Specific, platform-native action] — [why, based on data]
2. [Specific, platform-native action] — [why, based on data]
3. [Specific, platform-native action] — [why, based on data]
---
```

Be specific. "Post more Reels" is not an action. "Increase Reels to 5x/week and lead each with a contrarian hook — your top 3 posts all used this format" is an action.

End with: "Want me to build a new content strategy based on these findings?"
